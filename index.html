Aloha Adventures

Hi. I am Alina Aharonian and I am passion traveler. I love explore new places and make a memoroble photos. Since 2018 my heart truly belongs to Hawaii. I have fallen in love with its natural beauty, warm culture, and delicious cuisine.

Come with me and let us wander amongst the islands of Hawaii.

Hawaii is a breathtakingly beautiful destination located in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. This archipelago is made up of eight main islands, each with its own unique character and charm. Hawaii is known for its stunning natural beauty, from its white sandy beaches and crystal clear waters to its lush rainforests and towering volcanoes. It's a popular vacation spot for tourists from all over the world who come to relax on the beaches, explore the hiking trails, and immerse themselves in the rich cultural heritage of the islands.

"Hawaiian tropical forest".

In the beginning I would like to share with you an amazing nature of Hawai Hawaiian lush forest green mountains are a breathtaking sight to behold. These mountains are part of the volcanic island chain of Hawaii and are known for their vibrant green vegetation and diverse wildlife.

"Sun, Sand, and Surf".

Hawaii is world-renowned for its beautiful beaches. The islands have every type of beach imaginable: white, black, and red sand beaches; romantic bays and beach parks for the family.And the most important thing is that all the beaches are completely free, without any fences.

"Exploring the Delicious Intersection of Hawaiian Food and Dance Culture".

Hawaiian culture is rich and diverse, with a unique blend of Polynesian, Asian, and American influences. This cultural heritage is reflected in many aspects of Hawaiian life, including its food, dances, and traditions.

  • My favorite and most popular food in the Islands is Poke with tuna. Hawaiian poke and beer are two culinary delights that are sure to please any food and beverage lover.
  • Hawaiian dances, also known as hula, are an important part of Hawaiian culture and history. Hula is a traditional dance form that tells stories through movement, music, and chant.
